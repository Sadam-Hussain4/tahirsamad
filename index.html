The man who started his initial schooling from his native village & proved his excellence in educatioin as well as cricket. The man who improved his cricketing skills from far flung area with no facility at all. Playing in streets, & paddy fields, this man proved himself a good cricketer in & outside Lolab valley. The man who always used to top his class upto high school education,opted to tempt his luck in cricket. He always used to deliver speeches in schools, & taking part in extra co-curricular activities. He who used to win prizes in school debates & functions, kept himself high in cricketing, & volleyball too. He had given all his best with teams, he palyed. The man who is known for his calmness,defendable,and agressive style of scoring whenever needed,had won many MOM,& MOS awards. He is also known for his superman fielding,& had won more than 10 best fielder awards in different tournaments as well. He is a well known top order cricketer, who can bowl spin & can swing, with lot to explore yet. He had played with the well known teams of the valley like DMWCC, MCC, YMC, Green Boys Chepora, Friends Eleven Lolab, Muqam Challengers,DCC, & many more.
